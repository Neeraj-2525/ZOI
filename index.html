SELECTION CRITERIA FOR THE DONOR

  1. Age between 23 and 35
  2. Indian citizenship
  3. Valid Aadhar
  4. Need not be married
  5. Need not have child earlier
  6. Should undergo all medical tests for general health
  7. Normal hormone values and ovaries on ultrasound
  8. Free from Infectious diseases like Hepatitis B, Hepatitis C , HIV and syphilis
  9. No history of genetic or medical problems
  10. Karyotyping available on request should be normal
  11. Donors are not allowed to donate more than once
